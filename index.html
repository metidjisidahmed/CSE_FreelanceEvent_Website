FAQ

What is Freelance Journey ?

An event organised by CSE for the lovers of freelancing . It's a series of workshops and conferences where the participants learn from the experianced freelancers the basics of Freelance

Who is able to participate ?

Every person interested in Freelance have the possibility to participate. How can I participate ? You have to fill the form above to attend the workshops. About the conferences, all you have to do is to join us on our Facebook live.

Is it free ?

Yes, absolutely. The mission of this event is to contribute to the development of Freelance in Algeria, push students to implement skills and acquire some experience.
